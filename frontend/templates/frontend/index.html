About Civic Issue Portal

The Civic Issue Portal is a unified digital ecosystem designed to streamline interactions between citizens, Ward Members, and Department Heads. Built with absolute transparency in mind, it provides a direct, uninterrupted channel to report local infrastructure issues, monitor project timelines, and ensure efficient, prioritized resolution for a better, scalable governing tomorrow.

By embracing decentralization, we empower local governments to act swiftly and efficiently on grassroots feedback, fostering unparalleled civic growth aligning directly with priority municipal initiatives.

Core Principles

  • 100% Digital Transparency
  • Direct Escalation Routing
  • Real-time Ward Monitoring
  • Sustainable Localisation of SDGs

Platform Capabilities
