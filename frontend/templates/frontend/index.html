About Civic Issue Portal

The Civic Issue Portal is a unified digital ecosystem designed to streamline interactions between citizens, Ward Members, and Department Heads. Built with absolute transparency in mind, it provides a direct, uninterrupted channel to report local infrastructure issues.

By embracing decentralization, we empower local governments to act swiftly and efficiently on grassroots feedback, fostering unparalleled civic growth.

Core Principles

  • 100% Digital Transparency
  • Direct Escalation Routing
  • Real-time Ward Monitoring
  • Sustainable Localisation of SDGs
